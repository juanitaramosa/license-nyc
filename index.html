Bars and Restaurants Have Paid Thousands in Fines During The Pandemic

By Juanita Ramos

200 bars and restaurants in the five boroughs of New York City have faced fines and license suspensions for failing to comply with social distancing rules and restrictions imposed by the state.

Although restaurants and bars have been hardest hit by the pandemic, they have faced fines of more than $10,000 plus liquor license suspensions. Many have been able to pay but others still have suspensions in effect.
